Meet Our Team

Welcome to IAAAACAME

The International Association of African American Ambassadors to Africa is dedicated to transforming communities across Africa through education, healthcare, and sustainable development. Founded by Dr. Zyra McCloud, we work to bridge the gap between Africa and the Diaspora, creating lasting change through collaborative partnerships and grassroots initiatives.
