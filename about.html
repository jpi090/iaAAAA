Our Founder & President

Dr. Zyra McCloud is a visionary leader, educator, and advocate with over 55 years of experience in driving educational reform and global development. As the founder and president of IAAAACAME, she has dedicated her career to fostering partnerships, building schools, and advancing economic and healthcare initiatives across Africa, Nigeria, and the West Africa region.

Recognized with numerous honors, Dr. McCloud continues to inspire change, influence leaders, and improve lives globally through her unwavering commitment to education and community development.

Vision, Goals, Objectives & Collaboration

Vision: IAAAACAME envisions a united Africa and Diaspora, working hand-in-hand to achieve sustainable economic growth, quality education, healthcare access, and social justice.

Changing the Globe

The vision for Africa.

Goals: To rebuild Nigeria and Africa by reconnecting with African Americans, fostering youth empowerment, and creating sustainable opportunities in ministry, agriculture, technology, and trade.

Objectives: We are committed to transforming lives and communities through sustainable initiatives that go beyond meeting basic needs. Our vision is to provide access to decent and affordable housing, create meaningful job opportunities, and empower young, innovative individuals with entrepreneurship training and digital skills to thrive in the future of work. We also strive to expand access to higher education, establish modern healthcare facilities, teach young people how to use technology responsibly, and ensure clean, safe water for every household.

African Unity

The Mission in Africa.

In addition, we are dedicated to strengthening food security through agricultural development, advancing renewable energy solutions for sustainable living, and creating community centers that inspire leadership, innovation, and spiritual growth through ministry. By investing in people and infrastructure, we are not only addressing today's challenges we are laying the foundation for resilient, flourishing communities that will endure for generations to come.

Collaboration: IAAAACAME partners with global and local organizations to advance solar energy, waste management, housing, import/export, and capacity building. Together with institutions like Cal Clean Earth (CCE), we foster green development while uplifting vulnerable communities.
